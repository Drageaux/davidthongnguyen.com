CakeBook.io

You can log in with Facebook or Google, or just create a brand new CakeBook account.
CakeBook.io is an easy-to-use recipe book for cake lovers. Its theme and styling mimic the look and feel of a real book on your wooden kitchen table, giving you an immersive experience just from using a seemingly typical web application. CakeBook is my very own personal project built upon the cutting-edge framework Angular 2, and it is fully functional & live.
